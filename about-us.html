About Us

The mission of EcoTrails and the team behind the project

Our Mission

We aim to help students learn more about the destinations they can choose for the next semester, focusing on environmental impact and cultural insights.

EcoTrails is designed to provide a comprehensive overview of each destination, including its environmental practices, cultural significance, and travel tips.

We believe that understanding the environmental and cultural aspects of a destination is crucial for making informed travel choices.

Our Team

This project is presented to you by Marceau Bilger and Sarah Arnaud, members of the class P2-INT3.

We are passionate about travel, culture, and sustainability, and we hope to inspire others to explore the world responsibly.

Our goal is to create a platform that not only informs but also encourages students to consider the environmental impact of their travel choices.

We are committed to continuously improving EcoTrails and expanding our content to include more destinations and insights.

Thank you for visiting EcoTrails, and we hope you find our platform helpful and inspiring !

Contact Us
