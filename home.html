What our website is about

We all dream of going abroad, new cultures, landscapes, people, and countless opportunities. In our studies at EFREI we are given the opportunity of spending one semester abroad amongst over a dozaine of destinations.

But as good a opportunity as it is, it still comes with consequences. The world is getting more and more aware of the environmental concerns of such activities. This is what motivated us to make this website , to educate and help people in their choice of a destination while keeping their carboon footprint to a minimun.
